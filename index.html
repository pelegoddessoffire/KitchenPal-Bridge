Pork Tostadas with Salsa Verde

Crispy tostadas topped with seasoned pork and fresh salsa verde, a vibrant Mexican dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tomatillo
  • 1/2 head cabbage, shredded
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lb pork, cubed
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Turn broiler to 500°F. Place tomatillo and jalapeño on a baking sheet and broil until charred, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add half the onions, garlic, tomatillo, jalapeño, cilantro, sugar, cumin, lime juice, and water to a blender. Blend until smooth to make salsa verde.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook pork until browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Assemble tostadas: spread salsa verde on tortillas, add pork, cabbage, avocado, feta, and serve.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 2

Calories: 600 kcal per tostada
